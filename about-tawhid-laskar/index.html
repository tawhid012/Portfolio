Tawhid Laskar (Web Developer)

Tawhidur Rahman Laskar, popularly known as Tawhid Laskar, is a young web developer and tech entrepreneur from Hailakandi, Assam, India. He is known for starting his programming journey at a very early age and building modern digital products for businesses and educational platforms.

Tawhid began learning programming at the age of 11 in 2021. As a self-taught developer, he explored web technologies, design systems, and modern development frameworks while still continuing his studies at Paramount Senior Secondary School.

Today, he works as a full-stack web developer focusing on creating responsive, scalable, and performance-optimized websites and applications. His development philosophy centers around clean design, strong user experience, and high performance.

Professional Role

Tawhid Laskar is the Founder and CEO of Limux Agency, a digital services initiative focused on helping startups, local businesses, and creators establish a strong online presence. The agency specializes in modern website development, SEO optimization, and UI/UX design.

Through Limux Agency, Tawhid aims to provide affordable yet professional web solutions, especially for businesses and institutions that are just starting their digital journey.

Developer Journey

Tawhid started coding in 2021 out of curiosity about how websites and digital platforms work. Over time he developed practical skills in both frontend and backend technologies while building real projects and experimenting with new tools.

He gained recognition as one of the youngest developers from Assam to secure an internship with DevTown, a global developer education community.

Apart from building projects, he actively shares technical ideas and development insights on the DEV Community platform, where he writes about web development, programming concepts, and modern software architecture.
